Mornings in Wells

My favorite place in the world just might be Wells, Maine. My family has been going to Wells every year for the past 30 years (before I was born). This past summer, I wanted to capture the sunrise from the Jetty, which I had never seen before in my 20 years of existence.

So I woke up around 4am, 70D in hand, and headed out to the end of the Jetty where the waves were rising and nearly crashing into my camera, I sat out there for around an hour, captured much of the sunrise, and headed back in to make coffee and edit the video.

Waves

This 2-D animation project was created using Adobe After Effects and PiskelApp for the 2-D Animations.

Using the song "Waves" by Miguel (Tame Impala remix), my goal was to create a virtual world where teh character crosses over into the colorful, free world as the beat drops. I am a huge fan of music, and just like most people, we all have songs that make us feel free from any outside bindings and responsibilities in our lives. My goal was to capture the essence of this feeling.
